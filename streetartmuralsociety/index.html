Welcome to the official website for the Street Art Mural Society (SAMS)

About Us

We are a non-profit group, formed for the purpose of gathering artists of all colors, adorning our art on the walls of abandoned buildings that are sited to be demolished or otherwise unused. Our exhibitions are free, however, we work with other art organizations, as well as many charities, opening our art to the world, with the option to donate to our associated charities, as well as supporting us so we can continue to paint the world a better place.
